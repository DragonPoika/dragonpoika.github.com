Shadowforms

Shadowforms are more powerful, faster and surrounded by a large cloud. They can be found in dark areas, but when in light areas, they'll get really weak.

Blackened Heart

Shadowforms sometimes drop blackened hearts, which can be used in multiple rituals. Wonder what's the origin story?

Mechanical Altar

The mechanical altar is a new block that can be used to damage nearby mobs. Just power it and let it do it's magic!
